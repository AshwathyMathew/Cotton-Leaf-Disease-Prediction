Remedies for Curl Virus:

  • Remove and destroy infected plants to prevent the spread of the virus.
  • Use virus-resistant plant varieties whenever possible.
  • Control whiteflies, as they are the primary carriers of the virus.
  • Apply neem oil or insecticidal soap to reduce whitefly populations.

Tips for Preventing Curl Virus:

  • Maintain proper plant spacing to improve air circulation.
  • Regularly inspect plants for signs of whiteflies or other pests.
  • Use reflective mulches to deter whiteflies.
  • Practice crop rotation to reduce the risk of soil-borne diseases.
