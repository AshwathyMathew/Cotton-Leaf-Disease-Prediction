Remedies for Bacterial Blight:

  • Remove and destroy infected leaves to prevent the spread of bacteria.
  • Apply copper-based fungicides to control bacterial growth.
  • Ensure proper plant spacing to improve air circulation.
  • Avoid overhead watering to reduce leaf wetness.

Tips for Preventing Bacterial Blight:

  • Use disease-free seeds and resistant plant varieties.
  • Rotate crops to prevent the buildup of bacteria in the soil.
  • Disinfect gardening tools to avoid spreading bacteria.
  • Maintain proper soil health and nutrient levels.
